# SEO Action Plan

## Immediate blockers

1. Replace placeholder sitemap freshness for property pages with real source timestamps from backend data.
2. Increase unique content depth on `/du-an`, `/dat-nen`, `/cho-thue`, `/lien-he`, and `/tin-tuc` so those pages are not thin category/landing pages.
3. Add visible author/editor attribution and real update dates to article pages.

## Quick wins

1. Add `/llms.txt` describing the site, core URL paths, and preferred canonical domain.
2. Ensure production env has `SITE_URL` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` aligned with the chosen primary domain.
3. Add a short “featured picks / why this area matters / market snapshot” section above listing grids to improve context and internal linking.

## Strategic improvements

1. Extend real-estate schema beyond generic `WebPage` where supported by your data model.
2. Add production-level QA for Rich Results, PageSpeed Insights, and rendered canonical/meta validation after deploys.
3. Strengthen E-E-A-T across the news area with editorial profile, review process, and update transparency.
